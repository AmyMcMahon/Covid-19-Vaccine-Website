What is Covid-19?

COVID-19 is a pandemic. The virus that causes COVID-19 is mainly transmitted through droplets. It is mainly spread through close contacts, this is people you have been within 1 to 2 meters of. Covid-19 is easily spread through areas that are poorly ventilated and crowded. To protect yourself from the virus you should:

  • Wear a face covering like a mask
  • Adhere to social distancing
  • Wash your hands
  • Avoid touching your face
  • Use hand sanitizer
