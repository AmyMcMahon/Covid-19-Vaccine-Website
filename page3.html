Vaccine Roll-out Schedule

Covid-19 Information Vaccine Information Register for the Vaccine

If you are aged 44 to 69 you can now register for your free Covid-19 vaccine here

The Covid-19 vaccine is currently being rolled out in Ireland. The vaccines are being administered as soon as they arrive in Ireland and the at-risk groups are being vaccinated first.

What Groups can Register for the Vaccine?

  • Frontline health-care workers
  • People aged 44 and above
  • People aged 16 to 44 who are in the high-risk group
  • Pregnant women

Current Registration Schedule for People aged 40 to 44:

  1. Aged 44 - register on Wednesday 2 June, or any time after
  2. Aged 43 - register on Thursday 3 June, or any time after
  3. Aged 42 - register on Friday 4 June, or any time after
  4. Aged 41 - register on Saturday 5 June, or any time after
  5. Aged 40 - register on Sunday 6 June, or any time after
